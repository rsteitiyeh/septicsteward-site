About SepticSteward

Owned and operated by Trilot LLC

SepticSteward exists because septic advice online is either a pumping company's sales page or a forum thread from 2011. We're neither. We build plain-English guides, a pump-out calculator, and printable maintenance schedules for the roughly one in five U.S. households on a septic system.

Who we are

SepticSteward is built by the team at Trilot LLC, led by Rami Steitieh — building online since 1997. We are not a septic pumping company and we don't sell septic services. We research public sources (EPA, state health departments, manufacturer documentation), interview cost data against real published price sheets, and build the tools we wish existed.

How we work

Every number in our guides is checked against the source it cites at the time of writing, and every article shows its updated date. We use AI assistance in drafting; every article passes a human-reviewed fact-check gate before it publishes. When we get something wrong, we correct it and note the update.

What we're not

We don't give engineering, legal, or health advice. A calculator estimate is a planning tool, not a substitute for an inspection by a licensed professional. For anything involving permits, system failure, or contamination, call your county health department or a licensed septic contractor.
